Our Services

Retained C-Suite Search

PTS provides a fully retained, highly consultative search to bring top industry talent to our clients. We partner closely with executive stakeholders for C-Suite roles to understand the positions' rationale. PTS translates that understanding into a comprehensive search as an extension of our client's strategy.

Contained Executive Search

The core of any business is hiring and retaining highly experienced management teams. PTS offers a unique search model that builds commitment while sharing the risk to ensure a successful outcome for our customers, candidates, and PTS. Our unique technology approach provides our customers with an in-depth analysis of each candidate we present, ensuring an optimal hiring process.

Technical/Cleared Talent

We recognize that most of our customers should recruit 98% of their roles, but it is the 2% where PTS can be your go-to partner. We specialize in the "purple unicorns" technical roles such as IT, Shared Services, Medical, and Classified positions. From NATO Secret to TS/SCI Full Poly, we deeply understand the nuances of penetrating this niche talent base. We are very effective at bringing cleared talent to our customers.

Bid & Proposal Support

Finding the right Key Personnel for your bids can be the difference between winning and losing. PTS bolts onto your Capture teams to find the most compliant and compelling talent for your bids. We lead an intense recruitment campaign, conduct compliance checks, obtain market intelligence, and manage the Letter of Commitment process, allowing Capture leaders to focus on the capture, not the recruitment.

Diversity & Veteran Recruiting

The PTS team is a highly diverse organization concerning gender, religion, nationality, and ethnicity. Our team comprises thirty-five members across eleven countries in North America, Europe, and the Middle East. PTS is a Veteran Owned Small Business. We work closely with American Corporate Partners and other veterans' groups to help service members transition into the industry and actively coach military candidates from soldiers to generals.
