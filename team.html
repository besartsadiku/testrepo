Meet the team

PTS Backstory

PTS was cofounded in 2015 by Jake Frazer, a former U.S. Army officer and 24-year Veteran of government contracting. After serving in Germany and Bosnia as a Cavalry officer, Jake went into government contracting in the late 90s. In the Balkans, he worked for Brown & Root, the precursor to KBR. During his six years working in Europe, the U.S., and the Middle East inside one of the industry leaders, he observed the necessity of finding the right people for the mission.

For almost ten years, Jake was the co-owner and C-Suite executive for a medium- sized international supply chain company, a leading prime to the U.S. Government, and an outsourced partner for major primes in the contingency contracting arena. While supply chain was the mission, talent remained the apparent challenge. During this decade of working across the industry, Jake built a strong understanding of many government programs and significantly extended his professional network.

Jake set up PTS in 2015 specifically to focus on the challenge of talent in the government contracting sector. Initially, the strategy for PTS was to be a SaaS-based matchmaking technology platform. PTS reoriented that technology to support actual recruitment. Jake has built PTS into one of the "Top GovCon Recruiters" in the industry, according to Jim Garretson of Executive Mosaic.

PTS Team

Our approach to building the PTS team is to create a diverse ecosystem of experiences that aligns with the needs of our candidates and customers. We hand-select professionals with extensive backgrounds in operations, business development, shared services, and talent acquisition to provide depth and perspective.

Our team includes Program Managers, H.R. Executives, Solutions Architects, I.T. Technicians, Project Control Specialists, Finance Managers, Site Managers, Special Project Coordinators, and Senior Operations Coordinators. Our functional, geographical, and ethnic diversity allows us to understand better our customers' requirements and the qualifications of our candidates to ensure we get the best fit.
