Why PTS?

PTS is so much more than an exec search firm or an agency. We are consultative partners helping our customers solve the greatest challenge in the industry…talent.

Experience

With over 400 years of cumulative government contracting experience from the C-Suite to the field, our team knows the programs/agencies that drive our industry. We have mapped the competitors, and each of our 30+ team members bring our own network to our customers.

Technology

PTS is revolutionizing recruitment by leveraging disruptive technology to include video interview, personality assessment, chance of leadership success predictor, cognitive assessment, functional fit, and technical scorecards. Our “virtual assessment center” reduces time-to-hire by up to 70%, increases hiring quality, and cuts overall hiring costs. We have also invested heavily recruitment technology to include dozens of subscriptions to LinkedIn Recruiter, ClearanceJobs, Devex, XXXX, XXX.

Philosophy

Just like our hiring managers, we have built teams in these same markets…so we know the importance of hiring the right talent. We also understand the perspective of our candidates and strive to treat them like customers. Our philosophy towards empathetic recruitment personifies our core values of integrity, communication, respect, initiative, and added value.
