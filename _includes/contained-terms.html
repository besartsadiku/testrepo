Our Contained Search Model

PTS’ unique “contained search” model offers a refreshing, flexible alternative to the prevalent “contingent” and “retained” recruitment models.

Contingent agencies are not obligated to fill a mandate and tend to provide a high volume of resumes to see what might stick with limited investment in proper intake, candidate due diligence, and customer intimacy.

Retained search firms charge higher fees with a requirement for permanent exclusivity and upfront payments to start a mandate, thus subjecting the client to a high level of commitment and financial risk.

PTS’ “contained search” model allows us to commit to each mandate and invest resources ensure successful results without exposing our client to disproportionate risk.
